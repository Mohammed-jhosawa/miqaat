Moharram ul Haram

01 - Urus: Moulai Abdullah Saheb [a.q.] (Khambat)

1 Moharram Ul Haram - Moulai Abdullah Saheb [a.q.] (Khambat)

Moulai Abdullah Saheb [a.q.]
Vafat: 1 Moharram Ul Haram, Khambat
zaman: Imam Mustansir Billah ( a.s. ) [427-487]

  • Imam Mustansir Billah ( a.s. ) [427-487] na zaman ma aap ye Hindustan ma be ( 2 ) Dai ne Hidayat vaste mokla: Maulai Abdullah ane Maulai Ahmed.
  • Beve sahebo Gujarat na Khambat mukame muqim thaya. [ 467 H.]
  • Maulai Abdullah ye ghana Jain ane Hindu logo sathe behas kari, Mojeza bataya, ane hazaro boot parastio [ idol worshippers ] ne Islam no qalemo parhayo.
  • Kahevay chhe ke sava man  ( about 50 kg.) janoi [ A holy thread worn by Hindus ] utreli. Khambat ma aa misal  'Dine Haq' roshan thayu. Aa haq ni roshni Khambat si Patan, Sidhpur ane Kauka, Pisawara na Ilaka ma phelai. Aasre 25000 logo ye Dawat ne javab dido.
  • Saghla karta pehla Khambat na Kumbi cast na Kaka Akela, ane Kaki Akela iman laya. {Kuva no mojezo}
  • Te baad Naagar Cast na ghana logo ye javab dido. Patan na Raja Sidhraj Jaisinh ane ehna be vazir Tarmal ane Bharmal ni madad rahi. Ye sahebo ye bhi Dawat no javab dido.
  • Maulai Abdullah 1 Moharramul Haram na din vafat thaya. Aap na mazar Khambat ma chhe. [Urs 16 Moharram manave chhe.]  
  • Aap ye Vazir Bharmal na farzand Maulai Yaqub ne Hind na Vaali banaya. Dawat nu markaz have Patan ma qayam thayu.
  • Maulai Abdullah ni nasal ma Doat ane Hudud Fozalah thaya. Dail Ajal Syedna Qasim Zainuddin [r.a] ( 31 ma dai ) , Maulai Hasanfir bin Ali , ane bija hudud aap ni nasal ma si chhe.

02 - Urus: Syedi Khanji Feer Saheb A.Q (Udaipur)

2 Moharram Urus Mukasaire Dawat,


Syedi Khanji Feer Saheb A.Q
Vafat: 2nd Moharram al-Haram, 1118H. 1706 A.D
Age: 80 yrs
Zaman: 34ma Dai Syedna Ismail Badruddin si 37ma Dai Syedna Noor Mohammed Nooruddin R.A

  • Syedi Khanji Feer Saheb A.Q. Syedi Fakhruddin Shaheed A.Q ni nasaal ma si che.

  • Aap ye 34ma Dai Syedna Ismail Bdruddin si 37ma Dai Syedna Noor Mohammed Nooruddin R.A tak 4 doat khidmat kidi chhe.

  • Aap Syedna Ismail Badruddin Saheb R.A ni hazarat ma ilm leva aaya tha. Jahan Dai sabaaq parhavta hata, te jagah na najdeek ghani badbu (foul smell) aavti hati. Syedi Khanji Feer Saheb A.Q khud te jagah ni safai kidi. Dai ne khabar pari tevare gahna khush thaya.

  • ilm ma aaleem tha. ghana olomas ne taiyar kida. Tema Syedi AbdulQadir Hakimuddin A.Q. Syedi Lukmanji bin Habibullah A.Q ane Syedi Hasanji Badshah bhi hata.

  • Aap hamesha awwal waqt ma namaaz vaste masjid ma padharta, Azaan deta.

  • 30 varas tak aap ye ahemdabad ni amalat kidi. Aap ne Mukasir no rutbo hasil thayo.

  • Ahemdabad ma dushmano ye ghani taklif aapi. Aap ne qaid kida. Aap na farzand qaid ma wafat thaya. [1114H] ehva barik zaman ma bhi aap taat ane khidmat par qayam rahya.

  • Ahemdabad si aap Udaipur khidmat vaste padhara, ane 80 varas ni umare udaipur ma 2nd moharram 1118H. 1706 A.D aap wafat thaya. Aap ni qabr par Aalishan qubbat bana hua che.

  • Aap ni dikri fatema Behen, ane aap na pota ahemdji ni qabr aap na qubbat ma chhe.

    • Syedi Sheikh Feer Jamaluddin, (a.q.) bin Mohammed

    Wafat: 2 Moharram-ul-Haram, 1003 H. Jamnagar Zaman: 27ma Dai. Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a.

  • Syedi Sheikh Feer Jamaluddin, (a.q.) na maa saheba [Managori Baisaheba ( Shauhar- Firji bin Jaafar a.q., Madfoon- Udaipur) ] syedi fakhruddin shaheed r.a. ni nasal si chhe.

  • Aap nu watan Dungarpur hatu. 20ma Dai, Syedna Hasan Badruddin saheb r.a. na zaman [ 940 H.] ma aap Dhrangdhra padhara.

  • Ek arsa tak aap vahan rahya, ane 24ma Dai Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin Saheb r.a. na farman si aap Jamnagar padhara. Yahan Mumineen nu vasvanu have shuru thayu. Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin Saheb r.a. ye aap ne halaar [aaj nu Saurashtra] na vaali banaya.

  • Aap aalim tha. Aap nu laqab Jamaluddin thu. Syedna Hasan Badruddin r.a. si Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah [r.a.] tak 8 Doat ni aap ye khidmat kidi. Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah [r.a.] ye aap ne Rasul Hudud banaya.

  • Jamnagar ma aap Vali-e-Hind, Maulai Adam ane Maulai Raj na muavin rahya. Jamnagar ma aap nu makan aaj bhi maujud chhe, ane aap na naam si Mohallo aabad chhe. Mohalla ni masjid aap ye banavi chhe. Masjid na minara ajab shaan na chhe.

  • 998H ma aap Amirul Haj hata. Haj si farig thai, Ahmedabad, Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a. ni hajrat ma aaya, ane vahan si Jamnagar padhara, ane vahan aap wafat thaya.

  • Aap ni umar 100 varas ni hati.

  • Aap na Qubba Syedna Taher Saifuddin r.a. na zaman ma,1365 H. ma tameer thaya.

    • Vaali-e-Hind,
    Maulai Raj bin Moulai Hasan Shaheed (a.q.),
    bin Moulai Adam (a.q.)

    Vafat: 2 Moharram ul Haram 925 H.1509 A.D. [Urs 29 Moharram thaay chhe.] Zaman: 21ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Husain bin Syedna Idris r.a.

  • Moulai Hasan (a.q.) bin Moulai Adam (a.q.) na baad Maulai Raj bin Moulai Hasan Shaheed (a.q.), Hind na vaali thaya. Aap nu laqab Shujauddin hatu, ane aap sachej shajaaat ni misaal tha.
  • Aap no zaman Laeen Jaafar ni fitnat na sabab mehnat ane musibato no hato.
  • Ghana mumineen deen chhori chala gaya. Aap faqiri no kurto pehni Ahmedabad na Mohalla ma ghar ghar fari ne bheekh mangva jata. Je koi aapva aave tene kaheta ke mein koi faqeer nathi, magar tamne hidayat aapva vaste aayo chhun. Aap ye mumin ne samjhavta, hidayat deta, ane aap ni aa mehnat na sabab hazaro Mumineen deen taraf pachha vala.
  • Moulai Raj bin Dawood -Soni (a.q.) , ane aap na farzand Shaikh Jafar Saheb (a.q.), aap ni muavin ma rehta.
  • Dushmano ye aap par ghano zulm kido. Aap no maal, ghar ane asbaab saghlu looti lidu. Baytul maal chhini lido. Aap ne qaid kida.
  • 904 H. ma aa fasaad babat Yemen si 20ma Dai, Dail Ajal Syedna Hasan Badruddin (r.a.) no ek kagaz lakhayelo chhe, je ma aap ye Maulai Raj (a. q.) ne ghano hoslo aapo chhe.
  • Aakhir ma Dushmano aap par tuti para ane aap na tukre tukra kari dida. Aap 2 Moharram 925H. na din shaheed thaya. Aap na sabab Haq nu raj baqi chhe.
  • Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) aksar farmavta ke jo Maulai Raj na hota to apan Mumineen kahan hota? Maula Lukmanji bin Habibullah (a.q.) [ madfoon-Surat], aap ni qabr par Sajdo karta. Huzur-e-Aala (tus) aap ni qabr par sajdo kari ziyarat kare chhe.

    • 06- Urus: Syedi Mohammed Qazikhan (a.q.) (Kapadvanj)

    6 Moharram ul Haram - Syedi Mohammed Qazikhan (a.q.) (Kapadvanj)


    Syedi Mohammed Qazikhan (a.q.)
    Wafat: 6 Moharram ul Haram, Kapadvanj
    Zaman: 26ma Dai, Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a.

  • Syedna Dawood bin Ajabshah ane Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah r.a. na Zaman ma Kapadvanj na aamil hata.

  • Bewe Dai aap na haath par varamvar Kapadvanj padhara chhe. Kapadvanj na mumineen ye beve Dai ni ghani khidmat kidi chhe.

  • Aap ikhlas ane safaa na saheb tha. Hamesha Dai ni khidmat ma rehta. Syedi Khoj bin Malek Saheb na aap ustad hata.

  • Gujarat ma Sultan Muzaffar no zaman hato. Mumineen ne ghani takalif hati. Aap ye te duur karva ghani mehnat kidi hati.

  • Aap 6 Moharram vafat thaya che. Aap na Qubba Kapadvanj ma Syedi Khoj bin Malek na roza si aagal jaata aave chhe. baju ma masjid chhe.

    • 07- Urus: Syedna Ismail Badruddin (RA) (Jamnagar)

    7 Moharram-ul-Haram - Syedna Ismail Badruddin (r.a.) (Jamnagar)

    38ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
    Syedna Ismail Badruddin (r.a.) Sani
    bin Almoula Shekhadam Safiuddin a.q
    Vafat: 7 Moharram-ul-Haram, 1150 H.1737 A.D. Jamnagar

  • Syedna Ismail Badruddin (r.a.) Sani ni viladat Udaipur ma 28 Zilhaj ul-Haram, 1097 H. ma thai.

  • 1110 H. ma aap na valid Syedi Shekhadam ni vafat na baad, Syedna Kalimuddin (r.a.) ye aap ni kafalat kidi. Aap na ghana imtehan thaya.
  • Syedna Nooruddin (r.a.) ye aap par nas farmavi. Aap talabatul ilm par ghani shafaqat rakhta. Fajr ni namaz na baad Quran na daur ni rasam aap ye shuru kidi.

  • Zaman ni taklif na sabab aap ye Jamnagar muki didu, ane Mundra shaher ne aabad kidu. Vahan Daras banayu, Masjid ane haveli banavi.

  • Aap bimar thaya, ane Jamnagar tashreef laya. 7 Moharram 1150 H. 53 varas ni umare aap vafat thaya. Aap ni turbat mubarak Qubbat Badriyah, Jamnagar, ma chhe.

  • Aap na aqila (wife), Fatema Aaisaheba binte Mulla Ibrahim [sakin, Wankaner] ni qabr aa Qubbat Mubarak ma chhe, je Syedna Abdultaiyeb Zakiuddin (r.a.) [Burhanpur] ane Almoula Abde Moosa Kalimuddin na valeda chhe.

    • 10- Urus: Syedna Zoeb bin Moosa Alvadei, [R.A] (Yemen)

    10 Moharram ul Haram - Syedna Zoeb bin Moosa Alvadei, [R.A] (Yemen)


    Pehla Dai, Dail Ajal,
    Syedna Zoeb bin Moosa Alvadei, [r.a.]
    Vafat: 10 Moharram ul Haram, 546 H. 1151 A.D. Haus, Yemen


    THERE IS NO IMAGE OF 07- Urus: Syedna Zoeb bin Moosa Alvadei, [R.A] (Yemen)

  • Syedna Zoeb bin Moosa Alvadei, [r.a.] -22 Shabanul Karim 532 H. ma Moulatena Hurratul Maleka (r.a.) ye Imam Aamir (a.s.) ni raza mubarak si aap na matha par nas no taj pehnayo.
