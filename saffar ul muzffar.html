Saffar ul Muzaffar

01 - Urus: Dai-al-Mutlaq Syedna Ali (r.a.) (Sana'a, Yemen)

1 Safar-ul-Muzaffar - Dai-al-Mutlaq Syedna Ali (r.a.) (Sana'a, Yemen) Dai-al-Mutlaq Syedna Ali (r.a.)

najle Mazoon-e-Dawat, Moulai Husain a.q. bin Syedna Ali bin Hanzala r.a. Wafat: 1 Safar-ul-Muzaffar, 686 H. 1287 A.D. Sana'a, Yemen


  • Syedna Ali r.a. Mazoon-e-Dawat, Moulai Husain a.q. na farzand ane 6th ma Dai, Syedna Ali bin Hanzala na pota chhe.
  • Ilm ma aap si koi qadam na milave sake. Har qism na ilm ma aap maheer hata. Fazal ane Zohad ma aap ni misal koi nohtu. Ilm Nujum [astrology] ma khas maaheer [ expert] hata.

  • Syedna Taher Saifuddin r.a. aap ni zikar famavat hua ek risala ma farmave chhe ke Syedna Ali r.a. adal [justice] ane tauheed [ belief in one god] na ilm ma bhi maheer hata. Je aap ni najdeek aave tehne hidayat mili jay.

  • Aap na Jad [ grandfather] ni misal aap bhi ilm-ul-Hayaat ane ilm-un-Nujum ma maheer tha. Hojaj na ilm ma koi aap no muqablo kari saktu nahin.

  • Har mazhab na logo aap ni samne besi adab si vaat karta, ane potana mushkil sawalo na javab hasil karta.

  • Raat din aap Khuda ni ta'at ane bandagi ma guzarta. Logo na qazia [ problems] ma haq [truth] si feslo farmavta.

  • Aap ye logo ne Ahle Bayt ni valayat par jame kida, ane shariat ni pabandi vaste ghanu samjhayu. Logo ne masjid ma jame kari waez si temna dil ilm si tar kari deta.

  • Aap 1 Safar-ul-Muzaffar, 686 H. 17 march 1287 A.D. vafat thaya.

  • Aap ni Qabr Sana'a ma chhe, magar jagah maaruf nathi. Dawat na arsh par aap teen saal jeva rahya.

    • 03- Safarul Muzaffar - Syedna Ali Shamsuddin Saheb (r.a.) (Shareqa-Yemen)

    Syedna Ali Shamsuddin Saheb (r.a.)

    najle Dail Ajal Syedna Abdullah Saheb (r.a.)h1 18ma Dai, Dail Ajal,
    Wafat: 3 Safarul Muzaffar, 832 H. 1428 A.D. Shareqa-Yemen

  • Syedna Ali Shamsuddin Saheb (R.A) ghani chhoti umar ma Ibadat, Taqwa, Ilm, Jehad ane Fazal ma kamaal ne pohncha tha.

  • Aap na ayyam mehnat ane imtehan na hata. Aap na Zaman ma Hind ma Jafar ni fitnat shuru thai.

  • Aap Zimarmar ma muqeem thaya, magar Zaidio ye zulm kido, tarah tarah si aap ne sataya, magar aap sabar karta rahya. Aakhare Dawat ni ane Mumineen ni hifajat vaste Zimarmar no qillo sonpi ne aap Hisne Afeda padhara, ane vahan si chhelle aap Haraz padhara. Vahan si aap ye Shibam java no irado kido, jahan aap na farzand Syedna Idris (r.a.) muqeem hata.

  • Aap Shibam vaste nikla. Zamana ye ghano zulm kido tho, Jism ma ghani kamjori aavi gai thi. Aap aap na bhai Izzuddin Maad (a.q.) na saathe Shareqa utra, ane yahan 3 Safarul muzaffar 832 H. ma Khamis na din zawal na waqt aap wafat thaya.

  • Syedna Idris ne aap na wafat ni khabar pohnchi, aap te waqt sakhat barish ane havaa na bavajud Shareqa padhara. Haraz na gamo ma si logo mohti ginti ma aaya. ane ghano matam thayo.

  • Aap ni qabr mubarak Shareqa ma chhe. Aap na jivar ma aap na Mazoone Dawat, Syedi Ali bin Abdullah Shebani (a.q.) ane Mazoone Dawat Syedi Mohammed Asaduddin (a.q.) bin Idris bin Syedna Abdullah (r.a.) ni quboor chhe.

  • Al Hayyul Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Aqa (RA) ye aap ni qabr par ghana nafees Qubba banaya chhe, je Rozat Tahera ni mini copy jeve lage chhe. Aap ye 1428 ma iftetah farmayo. Qubba shareef no mahol ghano nafees chhe.

    • 04- Urus: Syedna Abdul Tayeb Zakiyuddin RA (Burhanpur)

    4th Safar-ul-Muzaffar - Syedna Abdul Tayeb Zakiyuddin RA (Burhanpur)

    41st Dai-al-Mutlaq Syedna Abdul Tayeb Zakiyuddin RA
    Wafaat : 4th Safar-ul-Muzaffar, 1200, Burhanpur
    Duration of Dawat : 7 years

  • Syedna Abdul Tayeb Zakiyuddin R.A. Daot-e-Badriyeen na chhatha (6th) Dai chhe.

  • Aap ni viladat 1147H. 1734 A.D wankaner ma thai.

  • Aap na bavaji saheb Syedna Badruddin R.A vafat thaya, tivare aap ni umr ghani chhoti hati. Syedna Ibrahim Vajihuddin R.A. ye aap ni tarbiyat ane kafalat kidi.

  • Syedna Ibrahim Vajihuddin R.A. ye aap ne Kathiawar na umur soupa.

  • Majdooh ni fitnat thai tivare aap jamnagar ma hata. Te baad aap syedna AlMoayyed R.A ni hajrat ma Ujjain aaya. Vahan Aap ne waez karva nu farman thayu, ane aap ye waez kidi. Ane Mumineen na aqaaed na haq na deen par jamavi dida.

  • Syedna Moayyed R.A. na baad aap dawat na arsh par mustawi tahya. Har khamees (thrusday) na din aap waez farmavta. Ashura na din Lagan (Rozu) vaste app ghani taqeed farmavta.

  • Fajr ni namaz na baad "Taqarrub" ni doa "Yaseen" ane "innallah" parhvani rasam aap ye jaari kidi. Te siva biji ghani rasam aap ye zinda kidi.
  • Aap 4th Safar-ul-Muzaffar 1200 H . Burhanpur ma vafat thaya chhe.

  • Aap na Qubba aap na farzand 42ma Dai, Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin R.A. ye banaya chhe.

    • 06- Urus: Syedi Abdeali Imaaduddin Bin Shaikh Jeeva Bhai (A.Q.) (Surat)

    6 Safar al-Muzaffar - Syedi Abdeali Imaaduddin Bin Shaikh Jeeva Bhai (A.Q.) (Surat) Mukasire e Dawaat,

    Syedi Abdeali Imaaduddin Bin Shaikh Jeeva Bhai (A.Q.)
    umr: 71 years
    Vafat: 6 Safar,1271H , Surat
    Zaman: 47ma Dai, Syedna Abdulqader Najmuddin (R.A)

  • Syedi Abdeali Emaaduddin A.Q. ye panch (5) doat ni khidmat kidi chhe.

  • Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin R.A saheb ye Syedna Izuddin R.A ane Syedna Zainuddin R.A na saath Syedi Abdeali Emaaduddin ne ilm intekhab kidu. Aap allama-e-zaman chhe. Aap na upar Syedna ni Khaas nazar hati.

  • Syedna Zainuddin R.A ye Syedna Mohammed Badruddin R.A ane Syedna AbdulQadir Najmuddin R.A ne aap ni taraf sonpa. Aap ye beve sahebo ne taiyar kida. ilm ni amanat ne nakal kidi.

  • Syedi Abdeali Emaaduddin A.Q. ye ghani madeh likhi chhe. Arabi,Lisanu-d-dawat ane urdu ma ye madeh mashoor chhe. Syedna AbdulQadir Najmuddin R.A ni nas na munkereen aap ni samne kai boli na saka.

  • Haqiqat na kitab, "Lubbul Lubaab" , aap na ala'a kitab chhe.

  • Aap ye 47ma dai Syedna AbdulQadir Najmuddin R.A ni 15yrs khidmat kidi. Aap ne Syedna AbdulQadir Najmuddin R.A ye Mukasir na rutba ma qayem kida.

  • Syedna AbdulQadir Najmuddin R.A jivare Haj par padhara, te waqt aap par nas kari ne padhara.

  • Te arsa ma Syedi Abdeali Emaaduddin A.Q. ye ek puri waez "Bismillah" ni maani par kidi, je bayan ghanu shaan nu hatu.
  • 48ma Dai, Syedna Husamuddin R.A ye bhi Syedi Emaaduddin si ilm hasil kidu che.

  • Aap Syedna AbdulQadir Najmuddin R.A na zaman ma vafat thaya. Aap ni umar 71 varas ni hati.

  • Syedna AbdulQadir Najmuddin R.A ye aap ne Syedna Zainuddin R.A na jivar ma dafan kida. Qabr ma Syedna AbdulQadir Najmuddin R.A, Aap khud utra hata, ane famava laga ke mane mara bavaji saheb ni lahad chumvanu sharaf thayu.

    • 08- Urus: Moulaya Khattab bin Hasan Hamdani A.Q

    8 Safar al-Muzaffar - Moulaya Khattab bin Hasan Hamdani A.Q

    Mazoon-e-Dawat,
    Moulaya Khattab bin Hasan Hamdani A.Q
    Shahadat:: 8 Safar 533 H.1139 A.D. Huzurus Shaam-Yemen
    Zaman: Pehla Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Zoeb bin Moosa r.a.


  • Moulaya Khattab bin Hasan Hamdani A.Q ni viladat 440 H. ma thai. Ej Saal ma Moulatena Hurratul Maleka (r.a.) ni viladat thai.

  • Aap "Saheb-e-Ashraaf" tha, allama benazir tha. Aap ye Haqeeqat na kitab likha chhe.

  • Pehla Dai, Dail Ajal Syedna Zoeb (r.a.) ye aap ne Mazoon-e-Dawat no rutbo bakhsho. Satar ni Dawat na aap pehla Mazoon chhe. Zuhoor ane Satar beve zaman aap ye dekha chhe.

  • Aap no dushman aap no bhai Suleman tho. Hamesha aap ni mukhalefat [ oppostion] par rahyo. Beve na darmiyan ghana jang thaya. Har jang ma aap galib rahya. [won the battles] Maidan-e-Razam ma aap ye Suleman ne bhala si halaq kido.

  • Suleman ni halaq na baad tehna farzando ne aap ye potani kafalat ma rakha. Ej bhatijao ye ek raate gaflat no waqt dekhi aap ne aap ni aaramgah ma zahar si Shaheed kida.

  • Moulatena Hurratul Maleka (r.a.) ni vafat na 6 mahina baad aap shaheed thaya chhe.

  • Aap ni umar 93 varas ni hati.

    • 09 - Urus: Syedi Taiyebbhai Saheb Zainuddin a.q. (Surat)

    9 Safar al-Muzaffar - Syedi Taiyebbhai Saheb Zainuddin a.q. (Surat)

    Mukasire Dawat,
    Syedi Taiyebbhai Saheb Zainuddin a.q.
    Najle Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Saheb r.a.
    Wafat: 9 Safar 1333 H.-1914 A.D. Surat
    Zaman: 50ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Abdullah Badruddin R.A

  • Syedi Taiyebbhai Saheb Zainuddin a.q., 49ma Dai, Dail Ajal Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin Saheb r.a. na farzand ane 51ma Dai, Dail ajal Syedna Taher Saifuddin Saheb r.a. na aap shafeeq bhai chhe.

  • Syedi Abdeali Mohyuddin a.q. na najdeek aap ane Syedna Taher Saifuddin Saheb r.a. ye ibtedai Taalim hasil kidi chhe.

  • Syedna Burhanuddin Saheb ye beve sahebo ne Hadiyat ma rafe kida, ane laqab ataakida.

  • Syedna Abdulla Badruddin Saheb (r.a.) ye aap ne Mukasir na rutba ma qayam kida.

  • 1333 H. ma 50ma Dai Syedna Badruddin Saheb (r.a.) bimari na sabab Waaz na kari saka, te waqt aap ne Waaz na farman kida. Aap bhi bimar tha, magar Dai na farman ne mathe uthavi aap ye Waaz kidi.

  • Ek mahina baad navjavani ma aap wafat thaya. Syedna Saifuddin Saheb (r.a.) te waqt Dumas ma hata. Aap na vafat no Syedna ne ghano sadmo pohncho che.

  • Aap ni qabr Qubba Izziyah ma aap na Ustad Syedi Mohyuddin Saheb (a.q.) na baju ma chhe.

    • 13- Urus: Moulai Adam bin Suleiman (a.q.) (Ahmedabad)

    13 Safar-ul-Muzaffar - Moulai Adam bin Suleiman (a.q.) (Ahmedabad)

    Vaali-e-Hind, Moulai Adam bin Suleiman (a.q.)
    Wafat: 13 Safar-ul-Muzaffar, 836 H. 1433 A.D. Ahmedabad
    Zaman: 19ma Dai, Dail Ajal, Syedna Idris bin Syedna Hasan r.a.

  • Moulai Adam bin Suleiman (a.q.) ni shaan ghani azeem chhe. Ta'at ane Dawat ni khidmat ma aap be-misaal chhe.

  • 15ma Dai Syedna Abbas (r.a.) si 19ma Dai Syedna Idris (r.a.) tak 5 Doat ni khidmat kidi.

    • Patan [Gujarat] ma Vaalii-e-Hind Maulai Ali (a.q.) pase si ilm hasil kidu. Aap Patan [Neharwali] ma muqeem [ resident ] tha.
  • 810 H. ma Sultan Ahmedshah ye jivare Ahmedabad shahar vasayu, tivare Maulai Ali (a.q.) ne Patan si Mumineen ne vahan vasavva vaste arz kidi. Maulai Ali (a.q.) ye Maulai Adam (a.q.), Maulai Khwaja Kalan (a.q.) ane Shekha Mujal na saath bija Mumineen ne bhi Ahmedabad mokla. Badshah ye aap saghla ni ghani karamat kidi.

  • Maulai Ali (a.q.) na baad Syedi Hasanfir Saheb (a.q.) Hind na vaali thaya, ane te baad Maulai Adam Saheb (a.q.) Hind ane Sind na vaali thaya.

  • Aap no zaman ajab saadat no zaman tho. Ahmedabad ma mumineen "Bohra" na naam si mashhoor tha. Masjido abaad hati, specially, Idd-e-Gadire Khum Ahmedabad ma ghani shaan si manavta. Atraaf na bilad si Mumineen hajar thata.

  • "Sakka"[Pani pilavnar servant] no vaqeo [incident] aap na zaman ma bano chhe. Yemen ma Mumineen ma imaan ni susti dekhi, Syedna Idris (r.a) ye Hind na Mumineen na imtehan vaste Sakka par umur ni raza mokli. Maulai Adam (a.q.) Hind na vaali tha, magar ta'at ni be-misaal farmani batavi, Sakka na pachhal namaz parhi.

  • Aap nu laqab "Shujauddin" thu. Aap ni umr 80 varas ni hati, magar aap na laqab muaafiq zaifee [old age] ma bhi aap himmat ni misal [example] tha.

  • Syedna Idris (r.a.) na Zaman ma, 13 Safarul Muzaffar 833 H. aap wafat thaya. Huzure Aala (tus), aap ni qabr par sajdo dai ne ziyarat kare chhe.

  • Aap na Qubba ni bahar Maulai Khwaja Kalan (a.q.) ni qabr ek chhatri ma chhe.

    • 14 - Safarul Muzaffar - Kaka Akela ane Kaki Akela Saheb (Khambat)

    14 Safarul Muzaffar - Kaka Akela ane Kaki Akela Saheb (Khambat)

    Kaka Akela ane Kaki Akela Saheb,
    Urs: 14 Safarul Muzaffar.--Khambat
    Imam Mustansir (a.s.) no Zaman [427-467 H.]

  • Maulana Imam Mustansir (a.s.) na Zaman [427-467 H.] ma aap ni raza mubarak si Hindustan ni taraf Dawat na elaam rafe thaya. Imam Mustansir (r.a.) ye Maulai Abdullah (r.a.), Maulai Ahmedshah (r.a.) ane Maulai Nooruddin (r.a.) aap teen sahebo ne Khambat mokla. Khambat si 7 Km duur Kavadra gam ni pase Sakarpur gam ma Kunbi Jaat na Kaka Akela na khet ma aap teene Sahebo utra. Khet ekdam sukai gaya hata, magar aap Sahebo na qayam si sarsabz thai gaya. Khet na kuva ma pani sukai gayu hatu, Maulai Abdulla (r.a.) ye kuva ma ek bhalo maro. Khuda na fazal si ane Imam-uz-Zaman ni raza ane doa mubarak si kuva ma mitha pani no chashmo jari thayo.

  • Kaka Akela ane Kaki Akela beve Iman laya. Saathe bija ghana Iman taraf aaya.

  • Kaka Akela ane Kaki Akela ni turbat par ghana nafees qubba chhe. Aas pas haal sahan ane garden ni tameer thai gai chhe.

  • Kuva na pani ma shifa chhe, ane zaereen teno faydo le chhe.

  • Aap no urs 14 Safar manavay chhe.

    17 - Urus: Moulai Sheikh Saheb (Ahmedabad)

    17 Safar-ul-Muzaffar - Moulai Sheikh Saheb (Ahmedabad)

    Al Maula,
    Khwaja Mohammed bin Ishaq Shaheed
    urfe "Moulai Sheikh Saheb"
    Shahadat: 17 Safar-ul-Muzaffar, 908 H. 1502 A.D.-Ahmedabad

    •  Vali-e-Hind na Moulai Sheikh Saheb muaveen [assistant] tha. 
    •  Jafar ni fitnat na waqt aap na saghla bhaiyo iman si firi gaya, ane apna vali ne muki dida.
     Teva waqt ma aap akela saabut rahya.
     Dushmano ye aap ne shaheed kida. 
    
    •  Syedi Khoj bin Malek Saheb (a.q.) aap na risala ma likhe chhe ke, aap ni qabr Ahmedabad na Kalupur area ma Mulla Shaikh na mohalla ma chhe. 
    Aap apna ghar maj dafan thaya chhe.
     Dushmano ye aap na mazar ni ghani waqt behurmati kidi chhe. 
    
    •  Aap "Maulai Sheikh Saheb" si olkhay chhe.
     Ahmedabad ni " Nav (9) Ziyarat ma ni aa ziyarat ek che. 
    
    •  Qabr ni chaare taraf ni jaali 50ma Dai Syedna Badruddin (r.a.) na Zaman ma bani chhe.
     Mohalla ma mazar na saame ni masjid aap ye banavi chhe, je haal Sunnio na qabza ma chhe. Kalupur mohalla na area na ghana mumineen roj ziyarat kare chhe. 
    
    •  Syedna Abdullah Badruddin (r.a.) na Zaman ma, aap ni shaan ma,
     Shaikh Khanbhai Afsar ek nazam ma likhe chhe: 
    •	Karishma aap ka mashhoor, Khaso aam mein hai beshak,
    
    •	Kisi aurat ko hota hai,Kalak jab meh ki shiddat ka,
    
    •	to dho kar Roze ki janzeer, Use paani pilate hai,
    •	Vahi ho jata hai sar marhala uski viladat ka.
    
    •	[Meaning...aap na mojeza mashhoor chhe. 
    when delivery pain starts for a pregnant woman, the water dipped in the janzeer [chain] at the mazar results into a painless delivery.]
    
    •	Moulai shaikh saheb na mazar haal navesar si banaya chhe. darvaza rt side na haal bana chhe.
    22 - Urus: Syedna Husain RA bin Syedna Ali RA (Sana'a, Yemen)

    22 Safar-ul-Muzaffar - Syedna Husain RA bin Syedna Ali RA (Sana'a, Yemen)

    8th Dai-al-Mutlaq Syedna Husain RA bin Syedna Ali RA Vafat: 22 Safar-ul-Muzaffar, 667 H. 1269 A.D. Sana'a, Yemen 

                •	Aap bija satra na pehla ane Aale Valid na teesra Dai chhe.
            •	Sana'a ane Zamarmar ma aap no qayamgah tho. Zamarmar ma Bab-e-Yaqut ni under Mohalla ma ' Daru r Risha' nam na mahel na najdeek aap nu aabai [Forefather's] makan hatu.
            •	Aap nii sakhavat ghani hati. Zaban ma mithas ane fazahat itni thi ke logo ajab thata. Chehra mohra ma aap aao na valid Syedna Ali r.a. jeva rupala, kadavar ane qadkama wala hata.
            •	Raat din aap ilm nashar karta. Aap ye Haqaeq na ilm ma "Risalatul Vahidah", "Risalatul Aqeedatul Mavhedeen", "Kitabul Aydah val Bayan", ni misal na saat aali kitab tasneef farmaya chhe. 
            •	Aap na zaman ma Baghdad ma Banu Abbas ni sultanate khatam thai.
            •	651 H. ma ap na Mazoon ane Manoos, Syedi Ahmed bin Syedna Ali bin Hanzala a.q. vafat thaya.
            •	Siyasi fasad na sabab aap Sana'a padhara.
            •	Aap na vafat na najdeek Suraj ane chand ne gharan thayu chhe. Aap ni umr 40 varas ni hati. Aap ni qabr Sana'a ma chhe, magar jaga maaruf nathi.
    27 - Urus: Syedna Mohammed Izzuddin Saheb RA (Zabeed. Yemen)

    27 Safarul Muzaffar - Syedna Mohammed Izzuddin Saheb RA (Zabeed. Yemen)

    23rd Dai-al-Mutlaq Syedna Mohammed Izzuddin Saheb RA
    Vafat:27 Safarul Muzaffar, 946 H.1539 A.D. Zabeed. Yemen

    • Aap Banu Valid na 17ma Dai, ane Yemen na aakhari Dai chhe.
    • Aap Masaar ma tashreef rakhta. Aap par ghana zulm thaya. Sarfuddin Zaidi ye aap ne ghana sataya chhe. Syedi Hasan bin Nooh (a.q.) na wafat na baad ehno julm ghano ziyada thayo. Aakhar aap ne Masaar no quilo sonpi devo paro.
    • Hind ni taraf aap ye vali mokla. Mumineen ye ehni taa't kidi. Gadeer na din Yemen na logo no imtehan kido.
    • Hind si chaar sahebo ye Yemen hijrat kidi. Ulum hasil kida, ane Dawat na rutba haasil kida.
    • Aa char saheb hata:
    Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin (r.a.)
    Syedna Jalal Shamsuddin (r.a.)
    Syedna Dawood bin Ajabshah (r.a.),
    Syedna Dawood bin Qutubshah (r.a.)
    • Je waqt aap ye Hajj no irado farmayo ane Zabeed tashreef laya,
    to Sarfuddin Zaidi ye aap ne piva na paani ma zehar pohnchayu.
    Aap ye paani chakhu, ne ehsas thai gayo ke paani ma zehar milava ma aayu chhe.
    Aap fauran Zabeed vapis vali gaya, ane Zabeed maj vafat thaya.
    • Aap ye Hind taraf Syedna Yusuf Najmuddin (r.a.) ni taraf nas mokli.
    • Rasulullah s.a. ye je Hind ni taraf je doa kidi hati, te aap ye Miyan Malek bin Firoz ne sunavi hati, ke Hind na logo mara bhaio chhe, je aakhari zaman ma aavse, ane andhara ma Dawat ne baqi rakh se.
    • Aap ni qabr Zabeed ma chhe.
